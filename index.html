Fire Emblem

A fantasy tactical role-playing game franchise developed by Intelligent Systems

This is the time line of Fire Emblem releases:

  • April 20, 1990: Fire Emblem: Shadow Dragon and the Blade of Light
  • March 14, 1992: Fire Emblem Gaiden
  • January 21, 1994: Fire Emblem: Mystery of the Emblem
  • May 14, 1996: Fire Emblem: Genealogy of the Holy War
  • September 1, 1999: Fire Emblem: Thracia 776
  • March 29, 2002: Fire Emblem: The Binding Blade
  • April 25, 2003: Fire Emblem: The Blazing Blade
  • October 7, 2004: Fire Emblem: The Sacred Stones
  • April 20, 2005: Fire Emblem: Path of Radiance
  • February 22, 2007: Fire Emblem: Radiant Dawn
  • April 19, 2012: Fire Emblem Awakening
  • June 25, 2015: Fire Emblem Fates
  • July 26, 2019: Fire Emblem: Three Houses
